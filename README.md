## Welcome

I am an Associate Teaching Professor of Mathematics at Northeastern University.
My GitHub is a home for open educational resources, computational textbooks, interactive course materials, and research-inspired learning projects in mathematics, statistics, probability, stochastic processes, linear algebra, machine learning, and the mathematical foundations of artificial intelligence.

My work is guided by a simple principle:

> Mathematics is a language for describing structure, uncertainty, computation, data, and intelligence.

I develop resources that connect rigorous mathematical thinking with computation, visualization, simulation, and project-based learning.

## Featured E-Book Projects (Graduate and Undergraduate Math Education in the Age of AI)

The following books are based on my teaching materials for the corresponding courses and incorporate new ideas for the age of AI. The books are built/created (not traditionally written, not simply generated) with the help of AI (mostly ChatGPT, some Claude). Most of them are graduate-level or preparation for graduate studies. All e-books include **Python lab** and **interactive visualization page** in each section.

## Teaching and Development Themes

My educational projects emphasize:

* mathematical foundations of artificial intelligence
* linear algebra for data science, machine learning, and computation
* probability, statistics, and stochastic modeling
* topology and geometry in data analysis
* simulation-based and project-based learning
* computational mathematics with Python and Jupyter
* interactive textbooks built with Quarto and browser-based tools
* open educational resources for modern mathematics education

---

## Research Interests

My research interests include:

* algebraic topology
* geometric and combinatorial group theory
* topological data analysis
* random graphs and random geometric graphs
* right-angled Artin groups
* cohomology jumping loci
* configuration spaces and braid groups
* applications of topology and algebra to data science and machine learning
