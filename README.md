# He Wang

Welcome to my GitHub profile. I create open educational resources, interactive computational books, and teaching materials in mathematics, statistics, probability, stochastic processes, linear algebra, machine learning, and AI foundations.

My work focuses on making advanced mathematics more accessible through computation, visualization, simulation, and project-based learning.



## Online Books and Course Projects

### Linear Algebra, Matrix Analysis, and AI

- Advanced Linear Algebra in the AI Age: Geometry, Computation, and Data ([GitHub-Repository](https://github.com/wanghemath/Book-AdvancedLinearAlgebraAI))  ([Book Site](https://wanghemath.github.io/Book-AdvancedLinearAlgebraAI/))   
  Graduate-level applied linear algebra, matrix analysis, computation, data science, and artificial intelligence.

- Linear Algebra Computation ([GitHub-Repository](https://github.com/wanghemath/Book-ComputationalLinearAlgebra))  
  Linear algebra with computation, algorithms, and applications.

- Linear Algebra Language ([GitHub-Repository](https://github.com/wanghemath/Book-LinearAlgebraLanguage)) ([Book Site](https://wanghemath.github.io/Book-LinearAlgebraLanguage/))   
  A visual, story-driven introduction to vectors, matrices, data, and AI.

## Teaching and Development Themes

I am especially interested in:

- mathematical foundations of artificial intelligence
- linear algebra for data science and machine learning
- probability, statistics, and stochastic processes
- topology and geometry in data analysis
- computational mathematics and simulation
- interactive textbooks and open educational resources
- Quarto books, Jupyter notebooks, and browser-based learning tools

## Research Interests

My research interests include algebraic topology, group theory, topological data analysis, random graphs, right-angled Artin groups, cohomology jumping loci, configuration spaces, braid groups, and applications of topology and algebra to data science and machine learning.
